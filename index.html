About Me

My name is Jade MacRury and I am a unifier, able to see the different strands in a situation, and reason out a way to draw them together. I believe every experience, no matter how mundane, brings something of value and that life needs to be experienced in order to be lived.

To this end, I have collected experiences. Thirteen years in customer service. Two years in official and eleven in unofficial but equally relevant IT support roles. I've been a leader, a teacher, a coach, a writer, an editor, a photojournalist, a student, a mentee, a researcher.

I'm an extensive traveller, a dreamer and a dreamcatcher - having gone to nearly every single country I've dreamed of visiting as a child and achieved nearly every single dream.

All of my experiences provide a rich tapestry of inspiration for all of my creations. If you are like me or you love what I create, hit me a message and let me bring your dreams alive.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer's PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is pixel-perfect match.
